Account Deletion

Last updated: April 5, 2025

If you wish to delete your account and associated data from FilmFlow, follow these steps:

Steps to Request Account Deletion

  • Send an email to romeotech99@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request".
  • Include your username and registered email in the request.
  • We will process your request within **7 days** and confirm via email.

Data Retention Policy

Upon account deletion, the following data will be removed:

  • Personal details (username, email, etc.)
  • App usage history
  • Other associated data

Some non-personal logs may be retained for security and compliance reasons.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, email us at romeotech99@gmail.com.
